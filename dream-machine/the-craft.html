The Craft

Paint is the last five percent. It can't be average.

Every stage exists to protect the one part of the job the rider actually looks at every time they walk into the garage.

How it's done

Five stages. No shortcuts.

01

Consultation

What the bike is for, what it needs to survive, and what "right" looks like for this particular machine, discussed before anything is stripped.

02

Preparation & repair

Plastic welding, tank welding and repairs, and blasting where needed. The finish is only ever as good as what's underneath it.

03

Colour matching

Forty years of matching original manufacturer colours on models now considered classics, alongside bespoke colour and design work.

04

Paint & graphics

In-house originated, single-layer printed graphics from our own archive, or a full design built from scratch for a one-off machine.

05

Inspection & guarantee

Every job is checked before it leaves, and guaranteed. Collection and delivery available nationwide, for panels or complete machines.

Specialisms

Two directions. One standard.

Restoration

Classics Paintwork

Dream Machine started life repairing tanks for models that are now modern classics, and that restoration experience still runs through the workshop. Original colour schemes matched and reproduced, panels repaired rather than replaced where possible, and a finish that holds up to close inspection at a show, not just a glance in the car park.

Competition

Racing Paintwork

Nearly four decades in motorcycle sport at the highest level. Dream Machine painted machinery has competed in World Superbike, MotoGP, British Superbike, world endurance racing and the Isle of Man TT, and the same team preps race liveries for privateers and national-level competitors.

Collection & delivery

We come to you, then we bring it back.

A national van delivery service to the motorcycle trade, extended to collection and delivery of loose panels or complete machines from a home or work address, subject to area and terms. Paintwork has also been exported throughout Europe.
