The styling house

We're not a bodyshop.
We're where a machine gets its identity.

Dream Machine has painted motorcycles, exclusively, since 1977, under the same private ownership, with many of the original team still on the tools. That kind of continuity teaches you something: paint isn't decoration, it's the part of the bike everyone actually sees, and the part that can't be average.

Whether it's a full restoration, a race replica or a design nobody else is running, the approach is the same. Precision first. Restraint where it matters. A finish that still looks right in ten years, not just on the day it leaves the booth.

01 · Restoration

Classics, done properly

Forty years of colour-matching models now considered modern classics. Tank repairs, plastic welding, and finishes that respect the original.

02 · Race Liveries

Grid-proven finishes

World Superbike, MotoGP, British Superbike, world endurance and the Isle of Man TT. Painted to survive a season, not just a photograph.

03 · Own Designs

Nobody else's colour scheme

In-house printed graphics from our own archive, and complete colour and design changes for riders who don't want the standard option.
